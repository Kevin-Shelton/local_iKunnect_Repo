Privacy Policy

Kunnect, LLC (“Kunnect,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you provide when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information and outlines your choices regarding its use.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in various ways to improve our services and support your experience with Kunnect. This includes:

  • Information You Provide Directly - When you create an account, engage with our services, contact us, or participate in promotions.
  • Automatically Collected Information - Such as website activity, device details, and browsing behaviour via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Third-Party Data - We may receive information from business partners or service providers to enhance our offerings.

We collect and process this data in a manner consistent with industry standards and applicable legal requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

Kunnect may use collected information for various business purposes, including:

  • Providing and Managing Services - Delivering and improving our products, services, and customer experience.
  • Enhancing Security - Maintaining the integrity and security of our systems.
  • Analytics & Optimization - Understanding usage patterns to refine our services.
  • Legal Compliance & Obligations - Adhering to applicable regulations and responding to lawful requests.
  • Marketing & Communications - Sending relevant service updates or promotional materials (where permitted by law and user preferences).

We balance our business interests with our commitment to privacy, and we take steps to ensure any data use aligns with expectations and best practices.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to help protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These measures include industry-recognized security practices, though no system can guarantee complete security. We continuously evaluate our security protocols to align with evolving standards and legal requirements. However, we encourage users to take precautions, such as safeguarding account credentials and exercising caution when sharing personal information online.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers & Partners - With trusted entities that assist in delivering our services.
  • Business Operations - As part of strategic business initiatives, including partnerships, mergers, or acquisitions.
  • Legal & Compliance Needs - To comply with laws, enforce agreements, or protect our rights, property, or users.

Any third-party relationships involving data are subject to reasonable safeguards to maintain privacy and security.

5. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, or support legitimate business operations. Retention periods vary based on factors such as account activity, business needs, and regulatory requirements.

Where applicable, you may have rights related to your personal data, including access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain uses. Requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis in accordance with applicable laws. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at helpdesk@ikunnect.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Kunnect uses cookies and similar tracking tools to enhance user experience, analyze website performance, and improve our services. These technologies may help:

  • Remember user preferences for a more personalized experience.
  • Analyze trends to optimize website functionality.
  • Provide relevant marketing and advertising (as permitted).

Users may adjust browser settings to control or disable cookies, though doing so may impact certain features of our website.

7. International Data Transfers

Depending on your location and the services you use, your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with differing privacy protections. We take appropriate steps to ensure that such transfers comply with relevant legal frameworks and that data remains protected in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Data Breach Response

While we strive to maintain the security of our systems, unforeseen incidents may occur. In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to user privacy, we will take reasonable steps to assess, mitigate, and, where appropriate, notify affected parties in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Kunnect’s services are not directed at children under a certain age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or industry practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
