Digital Recording

Kunnect provides digital call recording for all customers at no extra cost, ensuring you meet industry requirements and maintain a secure record of every interaction. Here's what our call recording solution offers:

  • Real-Time Storage: All recordings are stored on a secure, client-accessible FTP site in real-time and are available for 30 days. Customers can download recordings to their own repository at any time.
  • Three Recording Options: 100% call recording for outbound campaigns. 100% call recording for inbound campaigns. Agent-initiated call recordings with on/off capability.
  • Detailed Call Information: Each recording is stored with essential details such as the telephone number, date and time stamp, unique identifier, and campaign name for easy reference and compliance.
  • FTC Compliance: As call recording becomes an industry requirement mandated by the FTC, Kunnect's solution ensures you stay compliant, supporting your long-term success.

FTC Compliance

Navigating compliance with the Telemarketing Sales Rule (TSR) can be complex, but Kunnect provides an innovative solution to ensure your outbound campaigns stay within legal limits without sacrificing performance:

  • Compliance with Drop Rate Limits: We offer the ability to cap your outbound campaigns to the prescribed 3% drop rate limit, ensuring compliance with TSR regulations.
  • Maintained Efficiency: Unlike other systems that may increase wait times or reduce contacts per hour, Kunnect maintains average wait times below 30 seconds and ensures over 25 right-party contacts per hour.
  • Avoid Penalties: If you're using outdated, premise-based predictive dialers, you could be at risk of significant penalties. Kunnect's advanced dialer keeps you compliant while enhancing your dialing efficiency.
  • Proactive Compliance Support: Our system is designed to help you stay ahead of regulatory changes, protecting your business from potential risks and penalties.
